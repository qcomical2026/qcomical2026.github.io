QCOMICAL School 2026

on Quantum Programming Languages and their Semantics

After a successfull first edition in Nancy (France), the second QCOMICAL School will take place in Pisa (Italy) as part of the QCOMICAL project. The school will offer tutorials and lectures by leading researchers involved in the project, working on quantum computing with a focus on quantum programming languages and their semantics, fostering interaction across foundational approaches.

November 16 to 20, 2026 – Pisa, Italy

Venue

The school will take place at the Museum of Computing Machinery, part of Sistema Museale d'Ateneo of the University of Pisa, located on the area of the former municipal abattoir ("Vecchi Macelli").

Address:

Museo degli Strumenti per il Calcolo
Largo Padre Renzo Spadoni
56127 Pisa, Italy

Open in Google Maps

The Museum is accessible from via Nicola Pisano. Leaving from the main train station ("Stazione centrale"), by bus: line 1+, stop "Rustichello 1"); leaving from the central main bridge ("Ponte di Mezzo"), by bus: line 5, stop "Rustichello 2"). It is about a 5-minute walk from the Leaning Tower of Pisa.

Visa Information

Participants from outside the European Union may need a visa to enter Italy. You can check the requirements according to your country of residence on the official Italian Visas website.

If you require an official invitation letter to support your visa application, please contact the organisers after completing your pre-registration.
